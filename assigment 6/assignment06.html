About Maze Game with Hand Tracking

A brief overview of our interactive hand tracking maze game.

Design Process

The design process began with the aim of creating an interactive game that could be controlled through hand gestures. By leveraging the p5.js and ml5.js libraries, we were able to integrate hand tracking functionality that interacts with the game's maze in real-time. The game's layout was meticulously crafted to provide an engaging and challenging experience, ensuring that each level would bring a new sense of discovery and excitement. The end result is a game that is not only fun to play but also showcases the possibilities of integrating machine learning into web-based applications.
