Street Racer: Behind the Scenes

Inspired by classic arcade racing games, Street Racer was created to immerse players in a fast-paced world where reflexes and strategy combine. I wanted to bring a bit of nostalgia to the new age, sprinkling it with modern coding techniques. The design process started with the vision of a dynamic, yet straightforward interface, easy enough for anyone to understand, yet challenging enough to keep seasoned players engaged. The game mechanics are based on the balance between speed and strategy, making each gameplay experience unique.
