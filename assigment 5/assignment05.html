Project Overview

Design Process The design process was a blend of creativity and technical expertise. I began by sketching out the layout of the world, deciding on key features like pyramids, a Sphinx, and a mystical river. The challenge was to make these elements not just static models but interactive pieces that respond to user actions. Using JavaScript and aframe libraries, I crafted each element with attention to detail. The pyramids, sky, the river and the enigmatic Sphinx were designed to create a sense of wonder. Special attention was given to ensure that the experience was majestic, making the world memorable audience.
